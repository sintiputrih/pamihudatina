How did Nicole Eggert start her professional career?

The actress has a long career behind her. When Nicole was five years old, her mother, who worked at a modeling agency, entered her into a beauty pageant, which she won. Shortly after the competition, a representative from Johnson’s Baby Shampoo approached her to appear in a commercial.

Shortly after, in 1979, she made her film debut in the movie When Hell Was in Session. The American drama film Rich and Famous, directed by George Cukor and released in 1981, offered her a role. She had several roles in the early and mid-1980s, such as Dennis the Menace in Mayday for Mother, as well as guest appearances on Fantasy Island and The Clan of the Cave Bear.

Co-starring with Scott Baio in the 1987 sitcom Charles in Charge was when she first gained widespread recognition as an actress. She also had recurring roles in seasons three and four of the drama series Baywatch as lifeguard Summer Quinn.

She took a break from acting to launch an ice cream truck which she called a “family business,” suggesting that she was getting tired of the industry. The 46-year-old actress has accomplished a lot professionally and appears to have successfully raised her children on her own.
