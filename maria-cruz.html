Who is Maria Cruz’s Murder Suspect?

On the afternoon of September 1, 2023, at around 4:20 p.m., the Miami-Dade Police Department swiftly responded to distress calls regarding a shooting incident near the 9900 block of SW 165 Terrace. Upon reaching the scene, officers encountered a distressing situation.

They found an adult female (Maria Cruz) who had suffered gunshots. In addition to this heartbreaking discovery, they also located another adult male who died of a self-inflicted headshot.

In a commendable display of professionalism and urgency, Miami-Dade Fire Rescue personnel arrived promptly to provide medical assistance. The two injured individuals were immediately transported to Jackson South Medical Center, where their conditions were deemed critical.

Despite the dedicated efforts of the medical personnel, the female victim sadly succumbed to her injuries shortly after that. It’s worth noting that another adult female was present at the scene who, thankfully, escaped physical harm. Furthermore, law enforcement recovered the firearm involved in the incident.
