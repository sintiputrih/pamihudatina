Who is Alexandra Kay Married To -: Alexandra Kay, affectionately known as Lexi, has captured the hearts of many with her soulful country music. The Love Story Begins

Lexi’s love story began in her hometown of Southern Illinois when she met Indiana Touchette, lovingly called Indy, in high school. They hailed from neighboring small towns, Waterloo and Millstadt. Despite their humble beginnings, their love story was destined for greatness.

Their first official date happened in 2012, shortly after they graduated from high school. Lexi fondly remembers the little details of that day, like what Indy was wearing and how he cleaned his car immaculately. A trip to the movies turned into a memorable moment when Lexi playfully teased him about indulging in cookies.

Love Blossoms

Their love story blossomed rapidly, and just three months after their first date, Lexi and Indy decided to move in together. It was a learning curve for both of them. They were just 18 and 19 years old, taking their first steps away from their childhood homes. Indy, a hardworking union laborer, worked during the day, while Lexi bartended in the evenings.

Despite their love, challenges emerged. Lexi signed a record deal in 2013 and began traveling extensively for her music career. This led to them seeing each other only in passing, a difficult period for any relationship.

After just six months together, they made the tough decision to break up. However, they soon realized how perfectly they complemented each other.

Rekindling the Flame

Lexi, now 30, spent about a year apart from Indy during which she penned a heartfelt song titled “Somewhere Near Indiana.” The song expressed her emotions about their breakup and her enduring love for him. Little did she know that fate had plans to reunite them.

When Lexi and Indy got back together, the lyrics of her song proved prophetic. The refrain included the line: “I want one more kiss under a harvest moon.” That night, there was indeed a harvest moon, as if the universe was aligning itself to their love story.

Indy remarked, “It was meant to be, and we’ve been together ever since.”

Balancing Love and Career

Despite their busy schedules, Lexi and Indy have managed to stay in sync. Lexi’s music career took off during the pandemic, thanks to her viral coffee singalong series on TikTok. She now tours the country, bringing her music to fans far and wide.

Their relationship remains strong, a testament to their enduring love. Indy proposed to Lexi during Christmas in 2019, creating a magical moment at the Anheuser-Busch brewery in St. Louis. Knowing Lexi’s love for Christmas, he arranged for a surprise proposal in a Yuletide wonderland. A tour photographer captured the beautiful moment when Indy got down on one knee.

A Wedding to Remember

Their love story culminated in a heartfelt wedding. On September 25, Lexi and Indy tied the knot in a picturesque wooded ceremony, officiated by Indy’s brother-in-law, Tim Hastey, who was ordained by American Marriage Ministries. A reception for 220 close friends and family followed in a beautifully renovated white barn in Mount Vernon, Illinois.

True to her artistic spirit, Lexi wrote a song for the occasion, titled “From September to Forever.” However, she decided not to make a grand performance during the wedding. Instead, she performed the song in private for her newlywed husband. As she puts it, “I’m a sucker for a love song.”

Conclusion

In the spotlight of her music career, Alexandra Kay, aka Lexi, found love and companionship in Indiana Touchette, Indy. Their love story is one of perseverance, destiny, and the power of music. From their high school days to the present, they have weathered storms and celebrated love in all its forms.
