Neve Te Aroha Ardern Gayford is a celebrity child who is best known as the daughter of New Zealand politician, Jacinda Ardern. 

Jacinda is the current Prime Minister of New Zealand.

Her Parents

She was born to mother Jacinda Ardern and father Clarke Gayford. Her parents are yet to be married. 

However, they got engaged last Easter. The couple first met in 2012 at an awards event. 

The news of the love birds’ engagement surfaced the media when Jacinda was seen at a ceremony wearing a diamond ring on the middle finger of her left hand. 

When the prime minister’s office was asked about the ring, the spokesman confirmed that the duo was engaged. 

The couple has not yet thought of starting another phase of life as husband and wife. 

Meaning Of Her Name

The name meaning of Neve is Snow, and it is of Irish and Latin origin. 

The meaning of Te Aroha is The Loved, the Compassionate. 

Te Aroha is also the name of a rural town in the Waikato region, near Hamilton. 

It is the same city where her mother was born, raised, and educated. 

Her parents chose the name Neve because they thought she looked like a Neve. They also liked the name. 

Te Aroha is a name that the pair had decided earlier.
