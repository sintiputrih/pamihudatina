Is Gavin McHugh affected by Cerebral Palsy?

Yes, Gavin McHugh has Cerebral Palsy in real life, just like his character “Christopher” in 9-1-1. According to reports, actors without disabilities play more than 95 percent of characters with disabilities on television, but 9-1-1 defied that trend by casting Gavin and ensuring his full inclusion.

A cerebral palsy is a group of disorders that affect a person’s ability to move and maintain balance and posture, according to ccd.gov.
