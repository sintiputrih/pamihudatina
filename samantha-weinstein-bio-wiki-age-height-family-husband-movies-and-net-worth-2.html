Samantha Weinstein Biography

Samantha Weinstein (born Samantha Gail Weinstein) is a Canadian award-winning actress, singer/songwriter, and lead guitarist, best known for her roles as Heather in MGM’s remake of Carrie (2013), Audrey in Jesus Henry Christ (2011), and Josephine in the TIFF award-winning short film, Big Girl (2005).

As lead singer/songwriter and lead guitarist, Samantha founded the Toronto-based, garage rock band, Killer Virgins, in April of 2016.
