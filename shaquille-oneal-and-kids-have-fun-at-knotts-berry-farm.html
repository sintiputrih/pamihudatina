SHAQUILLE ONEAL AND KIDS HAVE FUN AT KNOTTS BERRY FARM

May 2024 · 1 minute read

NBA player Shaquille Oneal and his kids had a whole lot of fun at Knott’s Berry amusement park in Buena Park, CA last week. Shaquille tweeted the following about his adventure at the park:

“I’m at knots berry farms n my butts 2 big 2 fit in da seats on ride. ahhhhhh (dats me yellin).”

Shaquille O’Neal and his wife Shaunie Nelson are parents to Shareef, Amirah, Shaqir, and Me’arah, ages ranging from 8 to 2, in order, and Myles, 11. Myles is Shaunie’s child from a previous relationship and Shaquille has one daughter from a previous relationship named Taahirah, 12.
