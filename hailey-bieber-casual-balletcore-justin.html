Hailey Bieber Puts a Casual Spin on Balletcore for a Breakfast Date With Justin

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Hailey Bieber made the balletcore trend her own for a breakfast date with husband Justin yesterday.

The model incorporated ballet-inspired staples into her look, including a tiny black miniskirt, and black leather ballet flats worn with white socks. She put a casual, street-style spin on the ensemble, however, by pairing the skirt with a big classic white T-shirt and a crisp white dress shirt, which she wore open. Hailey accessorized with a large black leather shoulder bag and slim black sunglasses by Saint Laurent, chunky gold hoop earrings, and her diamond B necklace. The model wore minimal makeup and kept her hair in a straight bob, rather than slicking it back into her go-to ballerina bun.

Justin also wore a crisp collared shirt—his brown with white polka dots—layered over a white tank. He opted for his usual light-wash baggy jeans, white Nike sneakers, and a black backward baseball cap.

The Biebers held hands as they walked to breakfast.

BACKGRID

Hailey has championed the balletcore trend since its inception, and recently did a photo shoot promoting her new Rhode lip tints in which she wore a pretty pink ballerina minidress from Tory Burch’s Spring/Summer 2024 collection; the piece had a futuristic skirt that seemed to be held up by two parallel hoops. The model styled the dress with black ballet flats with straps at the top, worn with white ankle socks. She additionally wore gold jewelry, including shiny ball earrings and a ring, and had a little black bow adorning her half-up, half-down hairdo. She wore glowy, rosy makeup, including powder-pink shadow, and a glossy crimson manicure.
