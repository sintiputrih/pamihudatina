Juju Salimeni- Biography

Juju Salimeni was born on September 24, 1986, in Brazil, under the zodiac sign of Libra, and has Brazilian nationality. She is well-known for her profession as a fitness model, most notably for appearing on the cover of “Playboy” magazine.

Juju has been involved in various sports since an early age and has grown up playing soccer, volleyball, and gymnastics. Juju Salimeni- Professional Career

Juju’s figure had changed dramatically by the time she was 22, and she was already somewhat of an internet celebrity – her fame rose even more after she began working as a TV reporter for the “Panic On TV” show. The show helped her gain some popularity in Brazil, and she went on to become the primary presenter of the “Panic Delivery” show in 2010, this elevated Juju to the status of a true Brazilian celebrity.

After becoming an internet phenomenon and appearing on TV, Juju was able to launch her fitness modeling career as well, she was asked to multiple photoshoots and therefore signed with one of Brazil’s most prominent modeling agencies.

She has appeared on the cover of “Playboy” magazine and has been named one of the world’s sexiest women by “VIP” magazine. Juju has since posed for various periodicals and appeared in advertisements for well-known brands and companies.

Juju is currently attempting to get into the film industry as well, but so far her efforts have been futile.
