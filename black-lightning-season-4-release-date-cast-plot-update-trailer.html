January 2024 · 3 minute read

First of all, Black Lightning season 4 is going to happen. When Black Lightning season one came to the screen in January 2018, it brought a whole new story to the DC Comics superhero world. Black lightning showcases a superhero’s return who is also a family man and a respected figure in his community. This is a fact one of the ways the series got the attention of its audience.

Moreover, with the series being introduced into the Arrowverse series, we have to say there is more to expect. Hence, take a ride with us as we give you the latest information about season 4.

Black Lightning Storyline:

The series follows a high school principal Jefferson Pierce, who tries to maintain order in his school. Meanwhile, unknown to the community, Jefferson acted as a superhero named Black Lightning about nine years ago. 

To keep his family together, Jefferson retired from being a superhero. However, in the wake of a ruthless gang called “the 100”, Jefferson is forced to come out of the shadow and become Black Lightning once again.

Black Lightning Season 4 Release Date: When will it air?

While we thought season 4 might have been affected the current situation of things, we got an exciting update in May. The CW revealed that Black Lightning season 4 was going to premiere in January 2021 – stated by the network President. Furthermore, other related Arrowverse series, including Batwoman and The Flash are likely to premiere in January 2021. But in reality, Season 4 aired on 8 February 2021.

Black Lightning Season 4 Cast: Who will be in?

We have most of the cast returning for season 4 of the series, and there is a lot to see from them. However, while some cast will not be returning, new faces are bound to show up along the way in the fourth season. 

For now, the following cast is featured in the show, and here they are:

  • Cress Williams plays as Jefferson Pierce – Black Lightning
  • Nafessa Willaims as Anissa Pierce – Thunder
  • ‘Hubie Halloween’ star China Anne McClain plays Jennifer Pierce role
  • Christine Adam as Lynn Stewart
  • James Remar plays Peter Gambi role
  • Marvin “Krondron” Jones III as Tobias Whale.

Black Lightning Season 4 Plot: What happens?

In the finale of season 3, Jefferson together with Jennifer and Anissa’s efforts to end the ASA was a success. Hence, with the ASA out of the way, we expect a fresh new beginning in season 4 or something more of the same as we all know.

In the meantime, we also expect the criminal world to be under the leadership of Tobias Whale, which will be exciting to see. However, Tobias is like going to face some foes along the way, probably Lala and Lady Eve – we are looking forward to this! 

To cap it all, we expect more villains for Black Lightning to battle. Besides, something new as regards the Arrowverse. There are so many things to expect and you might need to brace yourself for surprises.
