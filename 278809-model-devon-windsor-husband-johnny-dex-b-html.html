Model Devon Windsor and Husband Johnny Dex Barbara Welcome First Baby

May 2024 · 3 minute read

American fashion model Devon Windsor and her spouse Johnny Dex Barbara recently welcomed their bundle of joy, and they are already doting on their little one as first-time parents.

The former Victoria's Secret star Devon Windsor took to her Instagram to announce that she and her spouse Johnny Dex Barbara welcomed their first child together. She captioned her post:

"Enzo Elodie Barbara! Johnny and I are so in love with our little girl! She joined us on September 8 at 4:58 pm. Weighing 6 lbs and 13 oz!”

Supermodel Devon Windsor at the 5th Annual Diamond Ball at Cipriani Wall Street on September 12, 2019 in New York (left), and with her spouse Johnny Dex Barbara and their new baby (right) | Photo: Shutterstock and Instagram/@devwindsor

BABY’S ARRIVAL

The 27-year-old new mom shared her daughter’s birth was the best day of her life and thanked her fans and followers for all the well wishes.

Windsor posted a heartwarming picture showing her cradling her newborn child, who was dressed in an all-white outfit, wearing a romper with a matching beanie.

Devon Windsor attends the 2020 amfAR New York Gala on February 05, 2020 in New York City | Photo: Shutterstock

Mom herself wore pink satin pajamas with the words "Enzo's Mommy" embroidered on the long-sleeved top and paired the look with an arm bracelet.

Instagram users went on the comments section to send their congratulatory messages, with one person commenting, "Congrats, babe!!! Loving the name, but I might be biased."

PREGNANCY NEWS

Windsor announced she was expecting back in March and wrote on Instagram that she was excited to become a mom, adding she has dreamt about motherhood her whole life.

The Missouri native said she and Barbara were grateful for their then-unborn child and thrilled about the new chapter in their lives as new parents.

The swimwear designer discovered she was pregnant after six months of trying. She previously revealed she had planned on having a natural birth in a hospital.

Windsor and her Cuban beau also plan on eventually having at least three kids. In an appreciation post by Barbara, he wrote on social media on August 19 that he is the luckiest man to share all his experiences with his lovely wife.

WEDDING ANNIVERSARY

Their pregnancy news came after they celebrated their first wedding anniversary. The couple got married on November 16 back in 2019.

For their anniversary, they commemorated their special milestone via sweet social media tributes. At the time, Windsor posted the pair's wedding video and captioned her post thanking her love for being her teacher, leader, rock, and best friend.

Meanwhile, Barbara shared on his Instagram that his spouse is the woman of his dreams, adding life is nothing without her and that he realizes daily that he is the luckiest man to have a woman like her in his life.
