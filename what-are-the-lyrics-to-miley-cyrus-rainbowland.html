What are the lyrics to Miley Cyrus Rainbowland?

July 2024 · 3 minute read

International popstar Miley Cyrus‘ Rainbowland is the third track on her Younger Now Album, which was released in 2017. She performed it alongside the legendary 10-time Grammy winner, Dolly Parton, who is also her godmother.

Recommended Videos

Rainbowland can be interpreted as a song about living harmoniously in a judgment-free world, and it’s delivered by Cyrus and Parton as a southern duet. Speaking with Taste of Country about the song in 2017, Parton described what the song meant to her.

“It’s really about if we could love one another a little better or be a little kinder, be a little sweeter, we could live in rainbow land,” Parton said. “It’s really just about dreaming and hoping that we could all do better. It’s a good song for the times right now.

It begins and ends with Parton leaving Cyrus a voicemail discussing the piece. At the start, the “I Will Always Love You” singer exclaims her excitement to collaborate with Cyrus by singing along with her over a cassette. And at the end, she shares some thoughts on her additions to the score.
