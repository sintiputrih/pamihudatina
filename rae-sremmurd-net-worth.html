Rae Sremmurd | Career

They founded hip hop band Dem Outta St8 Boyz at an early age. They used money from their part-time jobs to buy equipment and produce music.

The boys used to release their music via social media. They were so passionately engaged in music that they started slacking off from their school. As a result, their mother kicked them out, leaving them homeless.

So they began squatting in an abandoned house. They also began throwing parties in order to spread their music.

The release of the song “Party Animal” gave them their local fame and a much-needed boost to their music career.

Later they signed a deal with Mike Will Made-It’s record label EarDrummers.With this music label, they produced their signature album, SremmLife.

Their second album SermmLife 2, had their best single, the Black Beatles.

The popular duo has been nominated for numerous musical awards. They were nominated seven times in BET awards and won once for the Best Group.

They were nominated four times for Billboard awards and won once for Top Rap Collaboration.

Other than this, they were also nominated for International Dance Music Award, MTVU Woodie Awards, Nickelodeon Kid’s Choice Awards, and Southern Entertainment Awards.

Currently, the duo is focusing on making solo songs, and Swaee Lee has assured their fans that the group has not dissolved.
