Inside Miley Cyrus and Liam Hemsworth Date at the Saint Laurent Fashion Show in Malibu

July 2024 Â· 3 minute read
  • Miley Cyrus and her husband Liam Hemsworth both wore all-black outfits for their date night in Malibu for the Saint Laurent men's Spring/Summer 2020 fashion show.
  • The couple most recently rocked standout looks by the brand for the MET Gala last month.

Saint Laurent creative director Anthony Vaccarello brought Parisian chic to the oceanside town of Malibu, C.A. for his Spring/Summer 2020 menswear show on Thursday. The international runway show was the perfect setting for Miley Cyrus and Liam Hemsworth to enjoy a romantic (and super stylish) date night out.

Naturally, the couple sat front-row for Vaccarello's latest show as they watched models walk down the runway in breezy, effortlessly cool fashions with the ocean as their backdrop.

Phillip Faraone//Getty Images

Cyrus and Hemsworth both rocked edgy, head-to-toe black outfits for the occasion. Cyrus, who just dropped her EP She is Coming, rocked slim-cut, heavily distressed jeans, a black top, and a black and grey tweed blazer. She layered plenty of silver necklaces while her wrist and fingers were dripping in jewels. The "Nothing Breaks Like a Heart" singer accessorized with edgy black boots and a tonal handle bag.

Cyrus's glam was as rock 'n' roll-chic as her outfit. She embraced the beach theme and wore her blonde hair down in loose waves.

Hemsworth, who was recently in Isn't It Romantic with Priyanka Chopra and Rebel Wilson, took a style cue from his wife and rocked a textured, dark look. The actor donned a black jacket, embellished dual-tone top, black slim cut jeans, and tonal sneakers as he posed for the cameras next to Cyrus.

After the runway show, Cyrus posted a few photographs to her Instagram story and Twitter alongside her man.

Cyrus and Hemsworth, who tied the knot in a secret wedding ceremony in Tennessee, also rocked Saint Laurent to the 2019 MET Gala. Although Miley has previously attended the annual event, which benefits the Costume Institute, this year's installment marked the couple's first time attending together.
