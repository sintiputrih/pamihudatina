Human Barbie Valeria Lukyanova Goes Without Makeup in Bikini Selfie

April 2024 · 2 minute read

Proof that Human Barbie is, well, human! Valeria Lukyanova, a.k.a. Human Barbie, offered a revealing glimpse of herself without makeup in a bikini selfie posted to her Facebook page earlier this week.

The photo shows the 28-year-old Ukrainian model bare-faced and baring her much-talked-about bod in a black bikini that showcases her super-tiny waist and surgically enhanced breasts. Her waist-length blonde hair hangs down her back, and she appears to not be wearing her usual doll-eye contact lenses. See more pics of Human Barbie.

Lukyanova is a controversial pseudo-star, not just because of her appearance but also because of her bizarre opinions and comments. Back in August 2013, during an interview with The Daily Beast, she claimed she communicates with aliens. "We speak the language of light," she said. "I have learned a lot from my contact with them."

She has also said she is a teacher at the "School of Out-of-Body Travel," where instructors show students "how to leave their physical body and travel in their spiritual body, where you can visit any place on the planet and in the universe." And in the April issue of GQ, she revealed that the idea of having children "brings out this deep revulsion" in her and seems unnecessary.
