Is Vanna White married, and how many children does she have?

February 2024 · 2 minute read

As Wheel of Fortune gains traction for Pat Sajak’s retirement, many have questions about Vanna White, her personal life, and her next career move.

Recommended Videos

Vanna White has been on the show since 1982, even announcing her first pregnancy with then-husband George Santo Pietro on-air. The pregnancy — sadly — resulted in a miscarriage, but it does demonstrate the familial relationship that White has with the viewers. The couple was able to welcome two children; Gigi Santo Pietro and Nikko Santo Pietro before their amicable split in 2002.

The film producer and restauranteur is now married to producer Melissa Mascari and the two share a child; Andrea Santo Pietro.

Despite her divorce, White has reportedly been in a relationship since 2012. According to People, she and John Donaldson met through a mutual friend and have been together ever since. Donaldson owns his own construction and development company in Southern California.

It was recently announced that Ryan Seacrest will be taking over Sajak’s position as host on Wheel of Fortune. White has yet to announce any retirement plans but has expressed that she “can’t imagine” the show without Sajak.

As far as we know, White has no retirement plans and will continue to turn letters alongside Seacrest. Some fans are disappointed that White won’t be replacing Sajak as host and even White herself has “lawyered up” in hopes of a host-sized raise for upcoming seasons, according to The New York Post.

To stay up to date on Vanna White’s career and personal life follow her on Twitter, Instagram, or visit the official Wheel of Fortune website.
