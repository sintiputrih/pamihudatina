After retiring as a player, Mayo quickly transitioned into coaching, joining the New England Patriots’ staff as an assistant coach in 2019. Under the guidance of Bill Belichick, Mayo embraced his new role and made an immediate impact on the team’s defense.

“Coaching is in my blood, and being able to share my knowledge and experience with the players is a privilege,” Mayo said.

As the inside linebacker coach, Mayo played a pivotal role in developing the Patriots’ defensive unit into one of the league’s most formidable. His ability to break down opposing offenses and devise effective game plans has been instrumental in the team’s success over the years.
