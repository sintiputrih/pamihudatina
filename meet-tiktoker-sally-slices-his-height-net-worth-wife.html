Meet TikToker Sally Slices! His Height, Net Worth, Wife

September 2024 · 5 minute read

If there’s one thing people can’t say no to, it has to be Pizza. One particular pizza maker, Salvatore Mandreucci, recently had massive growth on the TikTok platform after people started loving his motivational content about owning a pizzeria in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Salvatore, who is popular by the name Sally Slices or CEO of Inspiration or the Pizza Guy, had 4 million TikTok followers as of this writing. Below is an article about this incredible man.

Sally Slices On TikTok

Sally goes by @sallyslices on TikTok. Before he became arguably the most famous Pizzaiola in the world, he was a normal man who worked for Marcello’s Pizza Grill.

“My whole life everybody always told me I can entertain, I can make a crowd laugh. I said to myself, ‘Let me follow up on TikTok. Let me start making videos. Maybe it will go somewhere, right,” Sal shared the story of why he started TikTok to abc7ny.com.

Sally came to TikTok with a goal. Although he started posting on TikTok in mid-2019, people began to notice him on the short-form platform after February 2021. On February 10, 2021, he posted a video with the text reading, “Guys I own a pizzeria in Philly /I’m trying to get big on here to share my beautiful pizza with the world.”

On August 22nd, 2021, Sally posted a video that had a text reading “Rock bottom will teach you lessons…” with the music being motivational and sentimental. This TikTok blew over the Internet, receiving more than 2.5 million plays and 300 thousand likes. Due to the success of this video, Sal decided to follow the same pattern for his future videos. In 2022, the majority of his videos were motivational.

Among all the motivational TikToks Sally’s made, his favorite one is: ‘People know the price of everything, but the value of nothing.’ In fact, it’s also one of his most popular TikTok with over 10 million views. Sally revealed that this quote resonated with him because he had a lot of struggles growing up.

When Sally started his TikTok journey, his only aim was to “spread his family’s pizza and get Dave Portnoy to his family’s pizzeria,”  and it is safe to say that he has accomplished both. In case you don’t know who Dave Portnoy is, he is the founder of the popular sports and pop culture blog Barstool Sports, who has more than 2.7 million TikTok followers himself.

After being tagged in Sally’s TikTok comment section for six months straight, Dave decided to try a hearty slice of Marcello’s old-fashioned tomato pie as well as its famous Corleone pizza.

How Much Is Sally Slices Net Worth?

By 2022, Sally Slices’ net worth was estimated to be below $500 thousand.

Sally has people travel from all over the world to meet him and snag a corner slice at Marcellos Pizza Grill, Hamilton Square, New Jersey. Sal’s sister said sometimes Sal’s immense popularity gets annoying when on a busy night people want to talk to Sal.

Sally Slices also has merchandise T-shirts, hoodies, and aprons now. You can buy Sal’s merch by visiting sallytothemoon.com.

In case you did not know, Sally is also a musician. Recently, he released his first single, A Man’s Mind, on Spotify.

While Sally has achieved a lot of things in a matter of a few years, he is not willing to stop. Sal hopes to parlay his Internet popularity into a film career.

Sally said, “…I will be on TV. I will be in Hollywood one day,” to abc7ny.com. While this means that Sal will be away from the family business, his parents are all in for their son’s dream.

Talking about Sally Slices’ dreams to be on TV, he recently collaborated with Syndicate Film Productions.

Sally Slices Age

Born on March 11, 1997, Sally Slices’ age was 25 as of September 2022.

Who Are Sally Slices Parents?

Talking about Sally Slices’ parents, they are Marcello and Margaret Mandreucci. They have been married since 1988.

Sally’s father, Marcello, was born and raised in Sicily, a town in Italy. He studied the style of pizza made in Corleone, Sicily as a young boy. When he traveled to America at the age of 16 with his parents, he used his knowledge of pizza-making to go into the pizzeria business.

Margaret, Sally’s mother, is an Area Manager at Arbonne and an employee at KW Premiere Real Estate according to her Facebook.

Sally also has a sister named Gabriella. According to her LinkedIn, she is a manager at Pizza-Grill.com. She also has a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Management and Employee Relations from Rutgers University. Gabriella tied the wedding knot in December 2019.

Sally Slices Wife

Sally Slices has a beautiful girlfriend, whose name is Rachel Olla. According to her socials, she currently studies at The College of New Jersey and lives in Hamilton Square, New Jersey. Also, she likes clothes a lot.

Making an Instagram post on May 10, 2022, Sally revealed to his followers that he and his sweet girlfriend went to see John Mayers live.

Sally Slices Height

Sally Slices is super tall and stands at a height of 6 feet 5 inches.
