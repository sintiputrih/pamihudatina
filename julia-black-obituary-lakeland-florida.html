On Friday, October 27, 2023, Julia Black, a Lakeland, Florida native and junior in the Harrison School for the Arts Orchestra program, abruptly passed away. At the time of her death, she was 16 years old.

What is Julia Black’s Cause of Death?

Black was supposedly the orchestra’s principal oboist and a gifted musician. She passed away after succumbing to sudden cardiac arrest caused by a pericardial infection. The residents of Lakeland were apparently shocked and saddened to know about her untimely death.

Where Will Julia Black’s Funeral Services Be Held?

Her parents, Jenny Rebecca and Norman Larry Black, Jr., her brother, Varn Michael Black and her friend, Calum Muldoon will miss her.

Black’s death notice said that the teenager was a caring person who always did the right thing. The teen’s family said that the funeral will be held at St. Paul Lutheran Church on Thursday, November 2, 2023.

How Did Julia Black’s Loved Ones React on Her Death?

Community members are in grief following the untimely loss of Julia Black, a Harrison junior who was regarded as an honor student and an active volunteer in her community. Lakeland High School shared on Facebook, “Always on our minds, forever in our hearts. Julia will forever be Nulli Secundus.”

Black was regarded as a devoted teammate on the Lakeland High School varsity volleyball team and a passionately kind adolescent. The St. Paul Volleyball camp in Lakeland honored the deceased adolescent, who was said to have been a girls’ volleyball coach since 2021.
