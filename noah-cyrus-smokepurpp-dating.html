Are Noah Cyrus and Rapper Smokepurpp Dating?

July 2024 · 1 minute read

Are Noah Cyrus and Smokepurpp (Omar Jeffery Pineiro) an item?

The 20-year-old singer and 23-year-old rapper were photographed holding hands while out and about on Thursday (July 2). The pair stopped at Sunlife Organics in Los Angeles with Cyrus’ pooch. Cyrus donned a black face mask while Pineiro had his face mask off for some duration of their outing.

The two first sparked dating speculation when they shared Instagram Stories of one another. On Thursday (July 2), Pineiro shared a video of him and Cyrus cuddled up on a couch while messing around with a dancing elf Christmas filter.

Watch the Instagram Story, below.

Cyrus previously dated rapper Lil Xan (Nicholas Diego Leanos). The two met after Leanos slid into her direct messages in February 2018, though Cyrus didn't see the message until months later. The two seemingly confirmed their relationship in August 2018 with their collaboration, "Live or Die."

By September 2018, they broke up. Leanos even claimed that the whole relationship was set up by their record label. However, Columbia records denied the claim.
