The Cast Of 'Vikings' Ranked By Net Worth

April 2024 · 6 minute read

In 2013 the historical fantasy drama Vikings premiered and immediately became super popular. In 2020 — after six successful seasons — the show wrapped up, however, a sequel titled Vikings: Valhalla streaming on Netflix. The show currently has two seasons with a third on the way. It's safe to say that fans across the globe can't get enough of the sagas of Ragnar Lothbrok — and there is no doubt the sequel will be just as successful.

Today we're taking a look at just how wealthy the cast of Vikings really is. The show was immensely popular on the History Channel, so it is not a surprise that many of its stars profited greatly from being a part of the series. From Travis Fimmel to Gustaf Skarsgard — keep on scrolling to find out just which cast member is the richest!

10 George Blagden — Net Worth $900,000

We're kicking the list off with George Blagden who played Athelstan on Vikings. Besides this role, George is best known for appearing in shows like Versailles and Black Mirror as well as movies such as After the Dark, Les Misérables, and How You Look at Me. Currently, George Blagden is estimated to have a net worth of $900,000, putting him in the lowest spot of net worths compared to his Vikings castmates.

Next on the list is Katheryn Winnick who portrayed Lagertha on Vikings. Besides this role, Katheryn is best known for starring in movies like The Dark Tower, The Art of the Steal, and The Marksman — as well as shows such as Wu Assassins, Big Sky, and Bones. Donal Logue who played on Vikings next on our list.
