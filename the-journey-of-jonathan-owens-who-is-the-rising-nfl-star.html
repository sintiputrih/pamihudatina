NFL safety, Jonathan Owens, is a football star with the Chicago Bears, and also happens to have a high-profile relationship with the four-time Olympic gold medalist Simone Biles. So, we take a look at the life and career of this phenomenal star who has proved his worth as a player off and on the field.


The Early Years and Football Career

Jonathan James Owens, born July 22, 1995, in St. Louis, Missouri, loved football at a young age. Then, at Christian Brothers College High School, he quickly carved out a reputation for being one of the most hard-hitting linebackers and was honored with the prestigious CBC Linebacker of the Year, foreshadowing his early immersion and dedication to the sport of football. Owens sharpened his skills in Missouri Western College for his college career where he played college football in 2013-2017 after completing high school.
Owens was a 2018 undrafted signing of the Arizona Cardinals, and his desire to play landed him in the perfect home. 


NFL Impact

The statistics speak for themselves, and they tell the story of what Owens. does on the field in the NFL. Through the 2023 season, his career totals included 227 tackles with two sacks, a forced fumble, two fumble recoveries, eight passes defensed, and one interception, showcasing his effectiveness on the field as a safety.
Owens' ability to change direction, his knowledge of the game, and his commitment to preparation have no doubt elevated him to stardom in the NFL. He rose from being undrafted and made meaningful contributions to several teams - a testament to his toughness and dedication to the game.


A Love Story: Simone Biles Relationship

Apart from his sports accolades, Jonathan Owens has made headlines for his romance with Olympic gymnast Simone Biles. The pair's relationship kicked off in March 2020, and on Feb. 14, 2022, they revealed they were engaged, with a lovely courthouse ceremony on April 22, 2023, in Houston, followed by a lavish bash in Mexico.
The pair have won over fans across the globe with the loved-up posts they share across social media as well as their red-carpet appearances and support for one another. The tale of their love, their unbreakable connection, the happiness they find in each other.


The Couple

Since the 2021 Tokyo Olympics, when Biles withdrew from several events, Owens has been a steadfast supporter of Biles throughout her struggles. From their Instagram stories to their tweets, the two stand by each other shoulder to shoulder in the highs and lows during matches to the public, showing the strength of their partnership both on and off the field. VIS is the embodiment of a relationship that is based on mutual respect and admiration, commitment, and motivation in terms of supporting each other to succeed in their future endeavors.


In the End

The story of Jonathan Owens - who went from a determined college football player to a standout NFL safety, and now, to an integral part of the love tale of Simone Biles - is a multifaceted one. For the heartwarming moments with Biles as well as his impact on the field, Owens is an example of discipline, love, and investment in the world of sports and non-sports-related relationships. As Owens grows in his football career and his bond with Biles, his story stands as a true testament to passion, determination, and love on the stage of sports.
In recognizing the accomplishments and individual moments of growth that Jonathan Owens has celebrated, we pay tribute to his athletic capabilities. As fans, we hope for many more great performances from Owens on the field and years of happiness for the power couple, Jonathan Owens and Simone Biles.
