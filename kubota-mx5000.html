Kubota MX5000 Price, Reviews, Specs, Engine Oil Capacity, Weight, Serial Numbers,  Attachments, History, Features & Pictures

The Kubota MX5000 Utility Tractor was marketed as an intermediate model between the M and L series line.

It was less expensive than the similar Kubota M4900 and used a more basic transmission and hydraulic system.

Kubota MX5000 Utility Tractor was equipped with a Kubota V2403 diesel 4-cylinder liquid-cooled 148.5 ci [2.4 L] engine, which delivers 52.2 hp [38.9 kW] power at rated rpm 2700.

Kubota MX5000 Utility Tractor had a Kubota 2.4L 4-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD, 2,866 lbs [1300 kg] Rear lift (at ends), hydrostatic power steering, 8 qts [7.6 L] Oil capacity, 8 forward and 4 reverse gears.

Kubota MX5000 Utility tractor attachments include Kubota LB702, Kubota LA852 front-end loader.
