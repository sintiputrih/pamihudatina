Tiffani Thiessen Is Not Having More Kids: 'We're Done!'

June 2024 · 1 minute read

Time out! Tiffani Thiessen and her husband, Brady Smith, don’t plan on having any more children.

Saved by the Bell: Where Are They Now?

“No! We’re done. We’re done,” the actress, 43, exclusively told Us Weekly at the UCLA Mattel Children’s Hospital’s 18th Annual Mattel Party on Sunday, November 5.

“Being a mom, it’s up and down, it’s a little bit of a rollercoaster,” she added at the Santa Monica event. “I have two and I feel very blessed. Yes, I have my hands full but there’s people out there with five or six so this feels very easy.”

’90s Nostalgia

The Saved by the Bell alum and Smith, 45, who tied the knot in 2005, are parents of daughter Harper, 7, and son Holt, 2. Last year, Thiessen joked about breast-feeding during an interview with Taylor Strecker on the SiriusXM radio show Wake Up! With Taylor.
